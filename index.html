Frequently Asked Questions

At Portmeirionmalaysia Roasted Bliss, we understand that you might have questions about our services, products, and policies. We've compiled a list of the most commonly asked questions to help you get the information you need quickly.

We are open Monday to Friday from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM and Saturday to Sunday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
Do you offer vegan options?
Yes, we offer a variety of vegan-friendly drinks and pastries. Please ask our baristas for recommendations.

Absolutely! We offer catering services for events. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to place your order.

Yes, we offer free Wi-Fi for our customers. It's perfect for those who need to work or study while enjoying their coffee.
